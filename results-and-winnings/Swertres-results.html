How To Claim Swertres Prize?

Claiming your Swertres prize is easy. If you win prize amounting to ₱5,000 and below you can claim it at any authorized Lotto outlet or at the PCSO branch offices near you. If you win more than ₱5,000 to ₱20,000 you can claim it at any of the PCSO branch offices or at the PCSO head office and if you win more than ₱20,000, you should go directly to PCSO main office, located at 605 Conservatory Bldg., Shaw Blvrd. Corner Princeton St., Mandaluyong city.
