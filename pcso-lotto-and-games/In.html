How to Franchise Jollibee

by Paul M
May 22, 2018

About Jollibee

There are more than 1000 Jollibee stores worldwide and more than half of these are operated by franchisees.

Jollibee operates more than 130 stores outside the Philippines and present in 10 countries – USA, Hong Kong, Brunei, Vietnam, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, UAE, and Singapore.

Qualifications of a Jollibee franchisee

Ano ang qualifications para sa gusto mag-franchise ng Jollibee?

Ito ang qualifications na hinahanap ng Jollibe sa franchisee:

  • Good community standing
  • Strong leadership and people-handling skills
  • Willingness to devote time in managing the restaurant
  • Can successfully complete the required 3-month full time training program.
  • Ability to fund the investment requirement.

…kung nasayo ang mga qualification na yan, congratulations pwede ka maging franchisee ng Jollibee!

Franchise Fee, Investment, Manpower, Expenses and ROI

Magkano mag-franchise ng Jollibee?

Ang franchise fee ay depende sa final na size ng restaurant at facilities na kakailanganin. Nasa mga ₱30 to ₱40 Million.

Ano ang kasama sa Investment?

Kasama sa ₱30 - ₱40 Million Investment ang construction ng restaurant, kitchen equipment and facilities, furniture and fixtures, air-con system, signage, and pre-operating expenses.

Sino ang magpo-provide ng manpower para sa restaurant?

Ia-assist ng Jollibee ang franchisee sa recruitment and training ng management team nya. Ang franchisee ang responsible para sa salaries and benefits ng employees nya habang nasa training.

Ano pang expenses ng franchisee na hindi kasama sa franchise fee / Investment?

Ang franchisee ang magso-shoulder ng gastos sa food ingredients, salary and benefits ng employees, rental fee at iba pang related expenses.

Jollibee franchise ROI (Return On Investment)

Ang Jollibee franchise ROI ay (5) five years according sa article/presentation ni Charina Bautista sa prezi.com two years ago, noong ₱15-30 Million palang ang franchise fee ng Jollibee. Sabi nya pa, ang pag-franchise ng Jollibee ay “..sound investment..wise decision!!!!” daw.

According naman sa Jollibee ang ROI at restaurant profitability ay depende sa several factors kagaya ng sales, market potential, investment at sa ability ng franchisee sa pag-control nya sa operating expenses nya.

Details of franchise fee / investment cost, return of investments and other franchising expenses will be discussed with the franchisee once his application has been approved.

Franchising Application

Paano mag-franchise ng Jollibee sa Pilipinas?

Mag-download at mag-print ng Franchise application form at siguraduhing kumpleto ang pag fill-up ng form. Mag-prepare ng proposal kasama ang mga sumusunod:

  • Letter of Intent – nakalagay yung exact address ng lugar kung saan mo gustong itayo yung franchise, exact address mo kung saan pwedeng padalahan ng mga documents o letter, contact numbers mo, etc.
  • Vicinity Map of Proposed Site – para ma-guide ang field personnel ng Jollibee na pupunta doon para mag-evaluate nung lugar.
  • Legal documents na magpapatunay na sayo talaga ang lugar na pagtatayuan ng franchise (optional lang).
  • Detailed resume or bio-data.

At i-send sa:

JOLLIBEE FOODS CORPORATION

Franchising Services Department
8th Floor Jollibee Plaza
#10 F. Ortigas Jr. Road
Ortigas Center, Pasig City 1605
