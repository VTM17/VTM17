PCSO Super Lotto 6/49


The Super lotto 6/49 was introduced in July 2000, it was initially played once a week, every sundays with a minimum jackpot prize of ₱8 million. Tickets costs ₱10 per standard six number combination.

Due to the overwhelming response of the Filipino people, the game is now played thrice a week. Every tuesdays, thursdays and sundays. The jackpot prize was doubled, from the minimum jackpot of ₱8 million it's now ₱16 million. Now, each standard six number combination costs ₱20.

How to play Super Lotto 6/49?

It's easy to play Super Lotto 6/49. Just go to any authorized PCSO lotto outlet and get a Super Lotto 6/49 playslip and mark 6 numbers from numbers ranging from 1 to 49 or if you prefer random numbers, the Lucky Pick (LP) function will generate a number combination for you. After that submit your playslip to the outlet operator and wait for your ticket. You will pay ₱20 for every standard 6-number combination play. Keep your ticket with you to claim your prize if you win.

  • Mark your chosen numbers only with a vertical line.
  • If an error is made, mark the "void" to indicate that board is cancelled.

Different System Play / System Bet is also available. Which you can choose 7 up to 12 numbers, and the system will automatically combine all the different possible combinations. The more number combinations you have, the better your chances to win millions!

  • Mark your chosen system under the "SYS" (keep it blank if you will play standard 6-number combination).

In just one visit in a lotto outlet, you can bet in advance for up to six (6) consecutive draws.
Example: If you mark "6 Draws" on your playslip, you will pay ₱120, that is for the current draw and the next five (5) consecutive draws.

  • Mark how many draw you want to play under "DRAW" on your playslip.

The cut-off time for playing Super Lotto 6/49 is 8:00 p.m.

To play this game, you must be 18 years old or above.

How to win in Super Lotto 6/49?

To win the Super Lotto 6/49 jackpot, you must match correctly all the six winning numbers in any order. But if you are not lucky to get the jackpot, don't be dismayed! because you still have chances to win prizes up to ₱70,000 by getting any 5, 4 or 3 out of the six winning numbers drawn, in any order.

How much can I win?

You can win a guaranteed minimum jackpot prize of ₱16 Million if you matched all the six winning numbers drawn. If you got 5 out of the 6 winning numbers drawn you can win up to ₱70,000. Up to ₱2,000 if you got 4. And ₱20 if you got 3, called balik taya. Take a look at the prize breakdown of each draw to see what you’ve won.

What if there are more than one jackpot winner?

Super Lotto 6/49 is a pari-mutuel game. Meaning if there are more than one jackpot prize winner, the prize money will be divided among the winners equally.

When there is no jackpot winner, said jackpot prize shall be added to the computation of jackpot prize of the next scheduled draw.

Where can I see the Super Lotto 6/49 results?

The draws is televise in PTV 4. You can also find the official and verified Super Lotto 6/49 results right here on the LottoInfo.ph website. Just head to the Super Lotto 6/49 draw results page. The winning numbers are posted once the draw results have been verified and confirmed. However, we still encourage you to visit the official PCSO website if you still want to verify the game results there.

Draws take place every tuesdays, thursdays and sundays at 9:00 p.m.

How do I claim the money if I win?

Whether you win the jackpot or one of the smaller prizes, claiming your winnings is that simple. If you win prizes amounting to ₱5,000 and below you can claim it at any authorized Lotto outlet or at the PCSO branch offices near you. If you win more than ₱5,000 to ₱20,000 you can claim it at any of the PCSO branch offices or at the PCSO head office and if you win the jackpot prize, you should go directly to PCSO main office, located at 605 Conservatory Bldg., Shaw Blvrd.
