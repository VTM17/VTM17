PCSO Grand Lotto 6/55


The Grand Lotto 6/55 is formerly the biggest lottery game before the Ultra Lotto 6/58 was introduced in 2015. It's easy to play and jackpot starts at a massive ₱30 million. The biggest jackpot ever won in Grand Lotto 6/55 was a huge ₱741 million. Won by a single winner, a balikbayan, in November 2010. That is the biggest jackpot prize in the history of lottery in the Philippines.

How to play The Grand Lotto 6/55?

Playing The Grand Lotto 6/55 is easy. Simply visit any authorized PCSO lotto outlet and get a Grand Lotto 6/55 playslip. You can choose your own six numbers (numbers from 1 to 55) and mark them on playslip or let the Lucky Pick function generate random numbers for you. Then submit the playslip to the outlet operator and get the ticket. For every standard 6-number combination play you will pay ₱20. Then wait for the draw result.

  • Mark your chosen numbers only with a vertical line.
  • If an error is made, mark the "void" to indicate that board is cancelled.

You can also play the different System Play / System Bet, which you can choose 7 up to 12 numbers, and the system will automatically combine all the different possible combinations. The more number combinations you have, the better your chances to win millions!

  • Mark your chosen system under the "SYS" (keep it blank if you will play standard 6-number combination).

In just one visit in a lotto outlet, you can bet in advance for up to six (6) consecutive draws.
Example: If you mark "6 Draws" on your playslip, you will pay ₱120, that is for the current draw and the next five (5) draws.

  • Mark how many draw you want to play under "DRAW" on your playslip.

The cut-off time for playing the Grand Lotto 6/55 is 8:00 p.m.

This game is only available to players 18 years of age and above.

How to win in Grand Lotto 6/55?

To hit the jackpot you'll need to match all six of the winning numbers in any order. Don't be discouraged when you don't get the jackpot. Because you can still win prizes by getting any 3, 4 or 5 numbers of the winning 6-number combination, in any order.

How much I can win?

There are four prize divisions. The first one is the jackpot prize. If you got the perfect 6 winning number drawn, you win the jackpot prize minimum of ₱30 Million. The second one, if you got 5 out of the 6 winning number drawn. You can win up to ₱200,000. If you got 4, you can win Up to ₱3,000 and ₱20 if you got 3, called balik taya. Take a look at the prize breakdown of each draw to see what you’ve won.

What if there are more than one jackpot winner?

The Grand Lotto 6/55 is a pari-mutuel game. Meaning if there are more than one jackpot prize winner, the prize money will be divided equally among the winners.

If the Grand Lotto 6/55 jackpot is not won, said jackpot prize shall be added to the computation of jackpot prize of the next scheduled draw.

Where can I see The Grand Lotto 6/55 results?

The draws is televise in PTV 4. You can also find the official and verified Grand Lotto 6/55 results right here on the LottoInfo.ph website. Just head to The Grand Lotto 6/55 draw results page. The winning numbers are posted once the draw results have been verified and confirmed. However, we still encourage you to visit the official PCSO website if you still want to verify the game results from there.

The draws were held every mondays, wednesdays and saturdays at 9:00 p.m.

How do I claim the money if I win?

Claiming your winnings is easy. If you win prizes amounting to ₱5,000 and below you can claim it at any authorized Lotto outlet or at the PCSO branch offices near you. If you win more than ₱5,000 to ₱20,000 you can claim it at any of the PCSO branch offices or at the PCSO head office and if you win the jackpot prize, you should go directly to PCSO main office, located at 605 Conservatory Bldg., Shaw Blvrd. Corner Princeton St., Mandaluyong city, for lotto ticket checking and validation. For more details about claiming your prize, click here.
