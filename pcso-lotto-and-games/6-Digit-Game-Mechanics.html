6Digit

How to Play

  • Go to any authorized PCSO lotto outlet and then get a 6-Digit Game playslip and mark your chosen six numbers (each digit from numbers 0 to 9) you can choose (6) six same numbers (Example: 1-1-1-1-1-1).
  • Submit your playslip to the outlet operator and wait for your ticket. You will pay ₱12 for every standard 6 Digit Game combination play.
  • To win the 6 Digit Game 1st prize you need to match correctly all six of the winning numbers in exact order.
  • You can also win prizes by getting the first or last five, the first or last four, the first or last three, or the first or last two, in exact order.

Prizes

  • 1st Prize (Match correctly all 6 numbers in exact order) - Win minimum of ₱150,000.00.
  • Match correctly the first (5) five or last (5) five numbers in exact order - Win ₱40,000.00
  • Match correctly the first (4) four or last (4) four numbers in exact order - Win ₱4,000.00
  • Match correctly the first (3) three or last (3) three numbers in exact order - Win ₱400.00
  • Match correctly the first (2) two or last (2) two numbers in exact order - Win ₱40.00
  • The 6 Digit Game 1st prize is divided equally among the winners.
  • If there are no 1st prize winner for a given draw, the 1st prize pool is added to the computation of the next draw's prize pool.

Your Chances of Winning

  • You can choose numbers from (0) zero to (9) nine. So you have 10 numbers to choose from, therefore, that's a 1 in 10 chance of picking the first correct number.
  • When you select your 2nd number, since you can choose the same number, you still have 10 numbers to choose from, that is also a 1 in 10 chance of picking the next correct number.
  • Let's multiply all the odds of getting all the (6) six correct numbers, 1/10 x 1/10 x 1/10 x 1/10 x 1/10 x 1/10 = 1/1000000.
  • That means you have 1 in 1,000,000 chance of winning the PCSO 6 Digit Game's 1st prize.
  • Want to know more about the PCSO 6 Digit Game? We have a detailed article about the PCSO 6 Digit Game.

Draw Schedule

  • The PCSO 6 Digit Game draw days are every Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday, televised live over PTV4 at 9:00pm.
