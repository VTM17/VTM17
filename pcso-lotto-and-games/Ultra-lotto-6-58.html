PCSO Ultra Lotto 6/58 How to play UltraLotto 6/58


The Ultra Lotto 6/58 is the biggest and the latest lottery game offered by the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PCSO). Giving players a chance to win a minimum guaranteed jackpot of ₱50 million. The Ultra Lotto began on February 8, 2015. The chances of winning the jackpot are 1 in 40,475,358.

How to play Ultra Lotto 6/58?

Playing Ultra Lotto 6/58 is simple. Go to any authorized PCSO lotto outlet and then get an Ultra Lotto 6/58 playslip and mark your chosen six numbers (numbers from 1 to 58) or if you want to play with randomly selected numbers, the Lucky Pick function will generate a number combination for you. After that submit your playslip to the outlet operator and wait for your ticket. You will pay ₱20 for every standard 6-number combination play.

  • Mark your chosen numbers only with a vertical line.
  • If an error is made, mark the "void" to indicate that board is cancelled.

Increase your chances to win with different System Play / System Bet. Which you can choose 7 up to 12 numbers, and the system will automatically combine all the different possible combinations. The more number combinations you have, the better your chances to win millions!

  • Mark your chosen system under the "SYS" (keep it blank if you will play standard 6-number combination).

In just one visit in a lotto outlet, you can bet in advance for up to six (6) consecutive draws.
Example: If you mark "6 Draws" on your playslip, you will pay ₱120, that is for the current draw and the next five (5) consecutive draws.

  • Mark how many draw you want to play under "DRAW" on your playslip.

The cut-off time for playing Ultra Lotto 6/58 is 8:00 p.m.

To play this game, you must be 18 years old or above.

How to win in Ultra Lotto 6/58? How to play UltraLotto 6/58

To win the jackpot, you need to match correctly all six of the winning numbers in any order. You can also win prizes by getting any 5, 4 or 3 numbers of the winning 6-number combination, in any order.

How much can I win? How to play UltraLotto 6/58

There are four prize divisions. The first one is the jackpot prize. If you got the perfect 6 winning number drawn, you win the jackpot prize minimum of ₱50 Million. The second one, if you got 5 out of the 6 winning number drawn you can win up to ₱280,000. Up to ₱3,800 if you got 4. And if you got 3 ₱20 called balik taya. Take a look at the prize breakdown table of each draw to see what you’ve won.

What if there are more than one jackpot winner? How to play UltraLotto 6/58

Ultra Lotto 6/58 is a pari-mutuel game. Meaning if there are more than one jackpot prize winner, the prize money will be divided among the winners equally.

When there is no jackpot winner, said jackpot prize shall be added to the computation of jackpot prize of the next scheduled draw.

Where can I see the Ultra Lotto 6/58 results? How to play UltraLotto 6/58

The draws is televise in PTV 4. You can also find the official and verified Ultra Lotto 6/58 results right here on the LottoInfo.ph website. Just head to the Ultra Lotto 6/58 draw results page. The winning numbers are posted once the draw results have been verified and confirmed. However, we still encourage you to visit the official PCSO website if you still want to verify the game results there.

The draws are held at 9:00 p.m. every Tuesdays, fridays and sundays.

How do I claim the money if I win? How to play UltraLotto 6/58

Claiming your winnings is easy. If you win prizes amounting to ₱5,000 and below you can claim it at any authorized Lotto outlet or at the PCSO branch offices near you. If you win more than ₱5,000 to ₱20,000 you can claim it at any of the PCSO branch offices or at the PCSO head office and if you win the jackpot prize, you should go directly to PCSO main office, located at 605 Conservatory Bldg., Shaw Blvrd.
