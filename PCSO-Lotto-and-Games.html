PCSO Lotto and Games?
It all started here.


The Philippines Charity Sweepstakes Office lauched the Lotto 6/42 in 1995. This is similar to the lottery games running in U.S., Europe, Australia. It is also known as lotto, it is thrilling and exciting game that gives entertainment and fun with an incredible prizes. In Lotto 6/42, the players need to choose any six-number combination, ranging from numbers 1 to 42. And wins when these numbers are drawn in any order during the draw date.

Since then, there are a lot of lottery games forms that have been added such as,

Digit games: the first digit game in the Philippines, launched in 1997 was the four-digit (4D) game. On the same year the six-digit (6D) game was also introduced. And after a half decade they launched the three digit (3D) game also called Suertres or Swertres. And two years after they released Suertres, the two digit (2D) came out or also known as Ez 2 lotto game.

Lotto games: the Mega Lotto 6/45 was launched in 1997. And after three years, they introduced the Super Lotto 6/49. And it was followed by the Grand Lotto 6/55 after a decade, where the biggest jackpot ever; won in the history of lotteries in the Philippines, an extraordinary prize costs 741 million. The latest lottery game was launched in 2015 was the Ultra Lotto 6/58.
